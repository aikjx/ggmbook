# Universal Mathematics vs Traditional Mathematics: 200 Lectures for Civilization Advancement

This is a comprehensive mathematics course with 200 lectures, helping you understand mathematics from a new perspective.

## Course Structure

- Lecture 1: How numbers grow
- Lecture 2: The nature of commutativity
- Lecture 3: The true meaning of multiplication
- Lecture 4: Origin of fractions
- Lecture 5: Decimal scales
- Lecture 6: Triangle interior angles
- Lecture 7: Mysteries of circles

$$y = mx + b$$

### Lectures 101-150: Advanced Mathematics

$$\frac{dy}{dx} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x+\Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x}$$

### Lectures 151-200: Interdisciplinary Applications

Apply mathematics to physics, AI, philosophy, and other fields.
