# Shushu Workshop

## Overview

Shushu Workshop integrates traditional Chinese mathematics with modern mathematics, exploring the intersection of ancient wisdom and contemporary science.

## Yijing Mathematics

The Yijing (Book of Changes) is not just a divination book, but a complete mathematical system.

$$\text{Taiji generates Two Forms}, \text{Two Forms generate Four Phenomena}, \text{Four Phenomena generate Eight Trigrams}$$

## Daodejing Analysis

The Dao gives birth to One, One gives birth to Two, Two gives birth to Three, Three gives birth to all things.

$$Dao = 0, One = 1, Two = 2, Three = 3$$
